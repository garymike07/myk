Mental Health App

About the Project

A comprehensive mental health tracking and support application with mood tracking, meditation guides, and professional consultation booking.

Technologies Used

  • React Native
  • Firebase
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • MongoDB

Project Goals

The goal of this project was to create a mobile application that would help people track their mental health and get the support they need. The app needed to be easy to use and provide a variety of features to help users manage their mental health.

Challenges and Solutions

One of the biggest challenges was to create a user-friendly interface that was both intuitive and engaging. We solved this by using a clean and simple design and by incorporating a variety of interactive elements, such as mood tracking and meditation guides. We also used Firebase to store user data and provide real-time updates.
